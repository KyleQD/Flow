## üëç Endorsement System

### What are Endorsements?

Endorsements are peer-to-peer skill validations where other users can vouch for your expertise in specific areas. They help build credibility and showcase your skills to potential clients and collaborators.

### How Endorsements Work

1. **Skill Assessment**: Users can endorse you for specific skills (1-5 star rating)
2. **Context**: Endorsements can be tied to specific projects, collaborations, or events
3. **Verification**: Endorsements can be verified by platform moderators
4. **Weighting**: Recent endorsements and verified endorsements carry more weight

### Skill Categories

- **Technical**: Audio engineering, mixing, mastering, live sound
- **Creative**: Music production, composition, arrangement
- **Business**: Project management, client relations, marketing
- **Interpersonal**: Communication, teamwork, leadership
- **Leadership**: Team management, mentoring, community building
- **Specialized**: Genre-specific skills, equipment expertise

### Endorsement Levels

- **1 Star**: Basic knowledge
- **2 Stars**: Some experience
- **3 Stars**: Competent
- **4 Stars**: Skilled
- **5 Stars**: Expert
